How to propose an event? 

There're two ways to do it
Email: Send your event details to ericjs.yu@mail.utoronto.ca. Please include the event title, description, date, time, and any other relevant information.
Discord: Join our Discord group and post your event idea in the #meet-up channel. Our vibrant community is always looking for new opportunities to learn and connect!
We're excited to see what amazing events you'll bring to our community!

If you wanna see current events, there's a little calendar button on top of the chatbot, click it and you will see all of events that being proposed!
