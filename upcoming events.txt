Event Proposal for the MScAC Community
Do you have an event, workshop, or seminar that would benefit the MScAC students and community? We'd love to hear from you! To propose an event, simply reach out to us in one of the following ways:
Email: Send your event details to ericjs.yu@mail.utoronto.ca. Please include the event title, description, date, time, and any other relevant information.
Discord: Join our Discord group and post your event idea in the #event-proposals channel. Our vibrant community is always looking for new opportunities to learn and connect!
We're excited to see what amazing events you'll bring to our community!
